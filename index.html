About Me

I am a full stack web developer at i.am+

I have experience with Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, Ruby, React JS, React-Native, Angular JS, Node JS and other technologies.

In 1998, I earned my BS Electrical Engineering degree from UCLA.

After seven years (1998 to 2005) as an integrated circuit layout designer at Vitesse Semiconductor, a partner and I co-found APM Jewelry in 2005.

I have always been fascinated by the mobile and web app revolution ever since the iPhone was released. In 2013, I'd decided to pursue my desire to become a web developer. In the Fall of 2014, I joined the General Assembly WDI program to learn the ins and outs of web development.

In March 2015, I joined i.am+ as a full stack web developer.

I enjoy working in smaller teams where members can work together and inspire each other on different projects.
